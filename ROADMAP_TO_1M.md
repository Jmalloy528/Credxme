## Brutal Truths (Read This Twice)

### 1. The First 6 Months Will Suck
- You'll work 60+ hour weeks
- You'll make little to no money
- You'll question if this was a mistake
- Most people quit here

**Solution:** Have 6 months of living expenses saved. Don't quit your job until month 4-6.

### 2. Content is a Grind
- Your first 50 YouTube videos will get <500 views
- You'll feel like you're talking to nobody
- Posting daily is exhausting
- Algorithms change constantly

**Solution:** Batch record. Hire an editor at month 3. Focus on helping one person per video.

### 3. Sales is Harder Than Marketing
- Getting leads is easy
- Converting them is hard
- You'll hear "I need to think about it" 100 times
- Objection handling is a skill

**Solution:** Record your sales calls. Review them. Read "SPIN Selling" and "Never Split the Difference."

### 4. Compliance is Non-Negotiable
- One CFPB complaint can shut you down
- State regulators watch credit repair closely
- One misleading claim = $10K+ fine
- Bond claims will destroy your reputation

**Solution:** Be paranoid. Review every ad with your attorney. Document everything.

### 5. Cash Flow Will Kill You
- Clients pay upfront (good)
- But you need cash to operate (bad)
- 90-day guarantee means delayed revenue recognition
- Affiliate commissions take 30-60 days

**Solution:** Keep 3 months operating expenses in reserve. Don't spend money you haven't earned.

### 6. Competition is Fierce
- Thousands of credit repair companies
- Many are scams (hurts everyone)
- Consumers are skeptical
- Google ads cost $10-50/click

**Solution:** Differentiate with education-first approach. Build trust before asking for money.

### 7. You'll Want to Pivot (Don't)
- "Maybe I should do crypto instead"
- "Real estate investing seems easier"
- "Dropshipping has better margins"

**Solution:** Commit to 18 months minimum. Most businesses fail from lack of focus, not bad ideas.
